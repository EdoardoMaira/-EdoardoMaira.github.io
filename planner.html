Personal Financial Planner

Project your net worth with a 5,000-path Monte Carlo simulation. All data comes from free Yahoo Finance pages; all calculations run in your browser and the report downloads as a PDF. Nothing is sent anywhere.

Expenses

Rent, bills, living costs — everything except investment contributions.
